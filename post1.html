Understanding Regression Analysis

Regression analysis is a statistical technique used to understand the relationship between a dependent variable and one or more independent variables. It is widely applied in fields such as economics, biology, and engineering to make predictions and identify trends. In this post, we dive deep into the methodology, assumptions, and applications of regression analysis.
