Understanding Bayesian Inference & Applications in Social Media Analytics

Bayesian models provide a flexible and robust framework for analyzing social media data and beyond. This article introduces the fundamental concepts behind Bayesian inference, derives the posterior distribution using Bayes’ theorem, and illustrates a practical example with Bayesian logistic regression for sentiment analysis.

Bayes' theorem is given by:

\[ P(\theta | D) = \frac{P(D | \theta) P(\theta)}{P(D)} \]

We also discuss extensions—such as hierarchical modeling, multiple predictors, and dynamic updating—and highlight further applications in event prediction, social influence modeling, macroeconomic forecasting, and clinical trial design.
